FAST FOOD APP

This is the design of a prototype of a food delivery app as part of a university project focused on improving the user experience. The main goal was to create an intuitive design that would facilitate navigation and improve the purchasing process. I used tools like Figma to develop wireframes and high-fidelity mockups, as well as usability testing to refine the design. This project allowed me to strengthen my UX/UI design skills, as well as better understand user needs through testing and interviews.
