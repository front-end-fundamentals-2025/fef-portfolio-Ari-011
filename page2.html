COFFE TABLE BOOK

I designed four pages of a coffee table book inspired by the Louis Vuitton collection created by Nicolas Ghesquière, highlighting its futuristic and structural approach. The project included the conceptualization of a visual design that captured the essence of his collection, combining modern and editorial graphic elements. I used Adobe InDesign, incorporating elegant typography and balanced compositions, as well as Photoshop for image editing.
